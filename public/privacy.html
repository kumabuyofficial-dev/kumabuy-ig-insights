Privacy Policy

KumaBuy IG Insights only analyzes Instagram data that a user provides directly or authorizes through an approved connection flow.

Data We May Process

Instagram username, account insights, media insights, captions, profile metrics, audience summary data, and generated reports.

How Data Is Used

Data is used to generate account diagnostics, content performance rankings, and marketing recommendations.

Data Deletion

Users may request deletion through /api/delete-data. A production database integration should delete stored records and revoke provider tokens.

Contact

For privacy requests, contact the KumaBuy team through the official website or Instagram account.
